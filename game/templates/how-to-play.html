How to Play

Rules

There are 50 level of questions. The questions will come in form of pictures. You have to turn those pictures into only a single word. Each word is illustrated by pictures.

Riddle me this is designed as a mixture of guess the word games and pictures trivia quiz to improve your creativity but still fun and rewarding. The more level is increased, the more complicated pictures will be. To solve tricky quizzes, You need to think outside the box! You can see game statistics down here.

Scoring

If you put a correct answer, you get score. If you are stuck, there is a hint for all questions. If you uses hint, we will give one letter of the answer and your score will be deducted. The score will not be deduct if you put the wrong answer.
